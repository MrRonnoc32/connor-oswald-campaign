Meet Connor Oswald

Connor is a dedicated public servant, a proud Floridian, and a father who believes in the power of community. After a decade of experience as a local teacher and community organizer, he's seen firsthand the challenges our families face.

He’s running for Congress to bring common-sense solutions to Washington, fight for economic opportunity, and ensure that every voice in our district is heard. He's not a career politician; he's one of us, ready to work for us.

The Issues That Matter

Economic Growth

Championing policies that support small businesses, create good-paying jobs, and lower costs for hardworking families.

Affordable Healthcare

Fighting to protect and expand access to quality healthcare, lower prescription drug prices, and defend reproductive rights.

Protecting Our Environment

Investing in clean energy and protecting Florida's natural treasures, from our coastlines to the Everglades, for future generations.
