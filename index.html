Meet Connor Oswald

Connor is a proud son of Jacksonville, a dedicated public servant, and a graduate of Darnell Cookman and Stanton College Prep. After teaching at Raines High School and Andrew Jackson High School, he’s seen firsthand both the challenges and the potential of our city.

He is running for the Florida House of Representatives, District 13, because he believes in the people of Jacksonville and in the power of community. Connor knows that the future of our city depends on strong schools, good jobs, and a government that works for the people—not special interests.

He’s not a career politician; he’s one of us—ready to fight for the working class, strengthen our neighborhoods, and ensure Jacksonville’s future is as bright as its promise.

The Issues That Matter

Economic Growth

Championing policies that support small businesses, create good-paying jobs, strengthen schools and lower costs for hardworking families.

Affordable Healthcare

Fighting to protect and expand access to quality healthcare, lower prescription drug prices, and defend reproductive rights.

Protecting Our Environment

Investing in clean energy and protecting Florida's natural treasures, from our coastlines to the Everglades, for future generations.
